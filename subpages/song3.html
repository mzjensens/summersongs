Song 3

Title: Not Like Us

Artist: Kendrik Lamar

Review:

As someone who doesn’t typically enjoy rap, "Not Like Us" didn’t do much to change my mind. The beat is solid, and the flow is impressive, but the aggressive tone and lyrics didn’t resonate with me. The message of standing out and being different is clear, but it’s delivered in a way that feels more confrontational than empowering. While I can appreciate the skill involved, it’s not a track I’ll be revisiting anytime soon.

Rating: 3/10
