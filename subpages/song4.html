Song 4

Title: Million Dollar Baby

Artist: Tommy Richman

Review:

"Million Dollar Baby" definitely has a polished, radio-friendly vibe with its catchy hooks and smooth production. The artist’s vocals are strong, and the upbeat tempo makes it easy to listen to. However, the song feels a bit too formulaic for my taste. It lacks the originality or emotional depth that would make it stand out. It’s a solid track, but it doesn’t offer anything particularly memorable.

Rating: $50/$1,000,000
