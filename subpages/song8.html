Song 8

Title: Lose Control

Artist: Teddy Swims

Review:

This song caught my attention right away, thanks to the artist’s voice, which really shines throughout the track. The vocal delivery is smooth and full of emotion, adding depth to the song. The beat is solid and the energy builds nicely. While the voice elevates the track, the overall composition doesn’t fully match its potential.

Rating: 5/10
