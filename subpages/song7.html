Song 7

Title: Please Please Please

Artist: Sabrina Carpenter

Review:

This song tries to tug at the heartstrings, but it ends up feeling a bit too desperate. The vocals are emotive, and the melody has a classic, pleading vibe, but the lyrics come off as repetitive and a little one-note. It’s clear the song is aiming for a deep emotional connection, but it doesn’t quite get there for me. It’s a decent listen, but it lacks the nuance that would make it truly compelling.

Rating: 1/3 please
