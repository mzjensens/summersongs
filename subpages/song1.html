Song 1

Title: I Had Some Help

Artist: Post Malone, Morgan Wallen

Review:

Though I consider myself a fan of country music I didn’t really enjoy this one. Though the music itself is good and both voices handle this style of music quite well, it’s in the lyrics that I find myself loosing interest. While the message, that it takes two to ruin a relationship, does resonate, the wording of it makes the whole situation seem irredeemable. Both parties sound like bad people. Not something I’m going to listen two more than 2-3 times.

Rating: 4 Beers/3 Chevy pickups
