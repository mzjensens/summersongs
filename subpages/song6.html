Song 6

Title: Too Sweet

Artist: Hozier

Review:

This song feels like a sugary treat you enjoy in the moment but forget about soon after. The melody is catchy, and the vocals are smooth, but it’s almost too polished, lacking the punch or originality that would make it stand out. It’s pleasant enough, but ultimately, it fades into the background.

Rating: 20 sugar packets/1 cup of coffee
