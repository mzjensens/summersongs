Song 2

Title: A Bar Song(Tipsey)

Artist: Shaboozey

Review:

This song has the makings of a good time with its upbeat rhythm and catchy vibe, but it falls short in leaving a lasting impression. While the energy is high and the party atmosphere is spot on, the lyrics are repetitive and lack depth. It’s fun for a few listens, but without a stronger narrative or emotional pull, it doesn’t stand out. A track that’s great for a lively night out but not one I’ll keep in my regular rotation.

Rating: 2/"to the 3, to the 4"
